Forman Course Scheduler

As a liberal arts university, we encountered the challenge of course registration, often leading to scheduling conflicts. To address this issue, we developed a website that utilizes web scraping techniques to extract data from our university's website. We then used BeautifulSoup (BS4) to parse this data. Our platform provides students with an intuitive interface to select their desired courses.

Once the courses are shortlisted, our system generates all feasible course combinations that are clash-free, free of duplicates, and align with the students' specific constraints and visualizes them. This was achieved by implementing a robust backend using Flask, and we designed the frontend with a sleek and user-friendly Bootstrap interface. This solution not only simplifies the course registration process but also enhances the overall academic experience for our students.

Gundogan Performance Dashboard

In our project focused on analyzing Ilkay Gündogan's 2020/2021 season, he emerged as one of the world's premier midfielders, showcasing exceptional skills in various aspects of the game. Leveraging data sourced from StatsBomb via FBRef, we meticulously examined his performance, comparing him to other midfielders to provide valuable context.

Gündogan's standout qualities included his remarkable safety in build-up play, his ability to execute progressive passes, and his keen eye for both creating goal-scoring opportunities and finding the back of the net himself. To provide a more comprehensive understanding of his performance, we developed custom metrics that delved deeper than standard FBRef data, offering a richer perspective on his contributions throughout the season.
